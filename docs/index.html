Privacy Policy

Ch'ti Pong does not collect, transmit or share any personal data. All your session data (duration, swing count, peak acceleration, score) is stored locally on your Apple Watch in a JSON file inside the app sandbox.

The app requests access to HealthKit only to start and end a workout session and to read your heart rate and active energy during the session. This data is used in real-time and saved by HealthKit, not by Ch'ti Pong. You can revoke this access at any time in the Watch Settings.

The app requests access to motion data (accelerometer) only during an active session, to detect swings. No motion data is stored or transmitted.

No third-party SDK is bundled in the app. No analytics, no crash reporting, no ads. Deleting the app deletes all data.

FAQ

Does Ch'ti Pong require an iPhone?

No. The app runs standalone on your Apple Watch. An iPhone is only needed once to install the app from the App Store.

Which Apple Watch models are supported?

Any Apple Watch running watchOS 26.5 or later (Series 6+, SE 2+, Ultra 1+).

Is my data sent to a server?

No. All data stays on your Watch. There is no account, no cloud sync, no analytics.

How accurate is swing detection?

The algorithm uses a 3g peak threshold and a 0.25s cooldown. Calibrated on real sessions. Reliable for clear strikes; gentle warm-up swings may be missed by design.
